
# Billion Dollar School Platform Strategy

## Market Opportunity
More than 1.5 billion students globally.

Schools require unified platforms for:
- communication
- learning management
- administration

## Business Model
SaaS subscription pricing.

Typical pricing:
$3–$8 per student per month.

Example:
School with 1000 students → $3,000–$8,000 monthly revenue.

## Expansion Strategy
Phase 1 – private schools
Phase 2 – international schools
Phase 3 – public districts

## Additional Revenue Streams
- payments
- analytics
- AI tutoring
- educational marketplace

## Vision
Build the global operating system for schools.
